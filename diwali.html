Happy Diwali!!!!


Deepavali or Diwali is an Indian festival of lights. 'Deepa' means lamp or light and'Vali' means string or row, and Deepavali means rows of lights. It celebrates the victory of good over the evil. Diwali falls on the day of 'Amavasyaa' or no moon day, usually in the month of October or November.The third day of Diwali is the main day of the celebrations. People worship Lord Ganesha and Goddess Lakshmi on this day and offer prayers to bless them with wealth and prosperity.
